Christian Jauvin¶

Independent software developer/consultant/researcher (M.Sc. in CS + Machine Learning, 2003, UdeM), specializing in scientific computing.

Ongoing Contracts¶

  • Botanical tree reconstruction from LIDAR point cloud data (Institut québécois d’aménagement de la forêt feuillue)
  • Gastrointestinal outbreak simulation on a CLUMEQ supercomputer (McGill Surveillance Lab)
  • Road accident address correction and geocoding (Direction de santé publique de Montréal)
  • Deep vein thrombosis (DVT) textual diagnostic classifier (McGill Clinical Informatics Lab)
  • Tuberculosis data management system (Montreal Chest Institute)
