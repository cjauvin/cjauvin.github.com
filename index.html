Christian Jauvin¶

Independent software developer / data analyst / researcher specializing in scientific computing (M.Sc. CS + Machine Learning, 2003, UdeM).

Ongoing Contracts¶

My current business model is to partner up with lab researchers in various fields to help them solve problems with computing techniques (machine learning algorithms, and other highly transferable skills), open source software and tools, and sometimes even come up with original research ideas and directions. Currently I’m working with:

  • McGill Surveillance Lab
    • Gastrointestinal outbreak simulation on a CLUMEQ supercomputer
    • Rotavirus vaccination data management system
    • NLP-based social media monitoring of vaccination refusal
  • Institut québécois d’aménagement de la forêt feuillue
    • Botanical tree reconstruction from LIDAR point cloud data (PypeTree)
  • Direction de santé publique de Montréal
    • Road accident address correction and geocoding
  • Montreal Chest Institute
    • Tuberculosis data management system

Past Projects¶
