Christian Jauvin
================

Independent software developer/data analyst/researcher specializing in
scientific computing (M.Sc. CS + Machine Learning, 2003, `UdeM
<http://www.iro.umontreal.ca/?lang=en>`_).

Ongoing Contracts
-----------------

My current business model is to partner up with lab researchers in
various fields to help them solve problems with computing techniques
(machine learning algorithms, among others), open source software and
tools, and sometimes even come up with original research ideas and
directions. Currently I'm working with:

* `McGill Surveillance Lab <http://surveillance.mcgill.ca/>`_

  - Gastrointestinal outbreak simulation on a `CLUMEQ
    <http://www.clumeq.ca>`_ supercomputer

  - Rotavirus vaccination data management system

  - `NLP
    <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_language_processing>`_-based
    social media monitoring of vaccination refusal

* `Institut québécois d'aménagement de la forêt feuillue
  <http://www.iqaff.qc.ca/>`_

  - Botanical tree reconstruction from `LIDAR
    <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LIDAR>`_ point cloud data (PyLIDAR)

* `Direction de santé publique de Montréal
  <http://www.santepub-mtl.qc.ca/>`_

  - Road accident address correction and `geocoding
    <http://cjauvin.blogspot.ca/2012/04/lean-geocoding.html>`_

* `Montreal Chest Institute <http://muhc.ca/mci/dashboard/>`_

  - Tuberculosis data management system
