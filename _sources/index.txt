Christian Jauvin
================

Independent **software developer / data analyst / researcher** specializing in
**scientific computing** (M.Sc. CS + Machine Learning, 2003, `UdeM
<http://www.iro.umontreal.ca/?lang=en>`_).

Ongoing
-------

My current business model is to partner up with lab researchers in
various fields to help them solve problems with computing techniques
(`machine learning <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning>`_
algorithms, and other **highly transferable skills**), open source
software and tools, and sometimes even come up with original research
ideas and directions. Currently I'm working with:

* `McGill Surveillance Lab <http://surveillance.mcgill.ca/>`_

  - PopHR: a `semantic web
    <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semantic_web>`_ application for the
    computation, management, querying and visualization of public
    health indicators (with Dr. `David Buckeridge
    <http://people.mcgill.ca/david.buckeridge/>`_)

  - Machine learning experiments for the analysis and classification
    of CIHR grant applications (with Dr. `Robyn Tamblyn
    <http://people.mcgill.ca/robyn.tamblyn/>`_)

* `Direction de santé publique de Montréal
  <http://www.santepub-mtl.qc.ca/>`_

  - Road accident address correction and `geocoding
    <http://cjauvin.blogspot.com/2012/04/lean-geocoding.html>`_
    (with Dr. Patrick Morency)
