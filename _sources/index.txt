Christian Jauvin
================

Independent software developer/data analyst/researcher (M.Sc. in
CS + Machine Learning, 2003, `UdeM
<http://www.iro.umontreal.ca/?lang=en>`_), specializing in scientific
computing.

Ongoing Contracts
-----------------

My current business model is to partner up with labs in various fields
to help them solve problems with computing techniques and open source
software and tools, and sometimes even come up with original research 
ideas and directions. Currently:

* `McGill Surveillance Lab <http://surveillance.mcgill.ca/>`_

  - Gastrointestinal outbreak simulation on a `CLUMEQ
    <http://www.clumeq.ca>`_ supercomputer

  - Vaccination survey application for a mobile platform

  - Deep vein thrombosis (DVT) textual diagnostic classifier 

* `Institut québécois d'aménagement de la forêt feuillue
  <http://www.iqaff.qc.ca/>`_

  - Botanical tree reconstruction from LIDAR point cloud data
    (PyLIDAR)

* `Direction de santé publique de Montréal
  <http://www.santepub-mtl.qc.ca/>`_

  - Road accident address correction and geocoding

* `Montreal Chest Institute <http://muhc.ca/mci/dashboard/>`_

  - Tuberculosis data management system
